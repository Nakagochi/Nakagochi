The Videos I Choose

I choose these videos because they are ones I have enjoyed. All of the creators and artists here are ones that I have come to enjoy over my time watching YouTube videos. They are all fun videos that either teach something new or make a person enjoy the time they are watching it. Bob Ross, in particular, is one of my hero's and shows how to find joy in life. Many people say they fall asleep to him, but I find him very calming.

AC/DC is one of my all-time favorite bands. That is why I added them to the table. I can't say that I am any sort of music head but I do enjoy their music immensely. I think this is one of the first bands that I could recognize their music and specifically say it was them. I have tried my hardest to learn band names and what their songs are because I have been called "uncultured" so much by my friends. I have the hardest time remembering facts about music because I am too busy enjoying the songs by themselves.
